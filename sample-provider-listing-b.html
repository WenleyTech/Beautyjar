About the Provider

Where clinical precision meets personalized care

Dr. Abby Sample is a board-certified dermatologist and Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology with over twelve years of experience in medical aesthetics, hormonal skin health, and evidence-based wellness. She founded Sample Dermatology & Aesthetics to bridge the gap between clinical dermatology and modern wellness — offering patients treatments grounded in science, delivered with care.

Her approach is built on thorough assessment, transparent communication, and individualized treatment plans. Whether managing complex skin conditions, guiding patients through GLP-1 therapy, or administering advanced aesthetic treatments, Dr. Sample believes every patient deserves a provider who listens first and treats second.
