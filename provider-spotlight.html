Provider Spotlight

Dr. Abby Sample on bridging dermatology and wellness

A board-certified dermatologist who believes the best outcomes start with listening — and that skin health, hormone balance, and confidence are more connected than most people realize.

MD, FAAD · Atlanta, Georgia · 12+ Years in Practice

BeautyJar Verified

Her Story

From clinical dermatology to a whole-person approach

Dr. Abby Sample spent the first eight years of her career in traditional dermatology — diagnosing conditions, prescribing treatments, and moving through a packed patient schedule. She was good at it. But something was missing.

"I kept seeing women come in with skin concerns that were clearly connected to something deeper — hormonal shifts, stress, weight changes, perimenopause. I could treat the surface, but I wasn't addressing what was actually going on," she says. "That's when I decided to change the way I practice."

In 2020, she founded Sample Dermatology & Aesthetics with a different model: longer appointments, comprehensive assessments, and a willingness to look beyond the skin. Her practice now spans medical aesthetics, hormone therapy, GLP-1 weight management, and clinical dermatology — all under one roof.

"I don't want to be the doctor who gives you a quick fix. I want to be the one who helps you understand what's actually happening — and gives you real options."
Dr. Abby Sample

Her Approach

Listening first, treating second

Walk into Dr. Sample's office and the first thing you'll notice is that she's not in a rush. Initial consultations run 45 minutes to an hour — significantly longer than the industry average. That's intentional.

"The consultation is where the real work happens. If I understand your history, your goals, and what you've already tried, I can build a plan that actually makes sense for your life — not just a menu of treatments I think you should buy."

Her patients describe her as thorough, direct, and refreshingly honest. She's as likely to talk someone out of a procedure as into one — a quality that's earned her a loyal patient base built almost entirely on referrals.

Her Services

What she offers

Dr. Sample's practice spans four core areas — each grounded in her belief that skin health, hormonal balance, and overall wellness are deeply connected.
