Onboarding

What We Need to Build Your Profile

The following information allows our team to create your verified BeautyJar profile. You'll review and approve the final version before it's published.

For office managers and practice staff: This checklist outlines everything we need. Most of it can be pulled from your existing website and marketing materials. If anything isn't readily available, just let us know — we'll work with what you have.

01
Provider Identity
Full Name & Credentials Required
As it should appear on the profile, including all post-nominal letters. e.g. Dr. Sarah Kim, MD, FAAD
Practice Name Required
The name of the clinic, practice, or medical group. e.g. Seoul Dermatology & Aesthetics Clinic
City & Country Required
Primary practice location as it should appear on the profile. e.g. Gangnam, Seoul, South Korea
Professional Headshot Required
A high-resolution, professional photograph. Clean clinical or editorial aesthetic preferred. Minimum 800×1000px. Please send as a JPG or PNG attachment. This photo will be the primary image on your profile.
02
Credentials & Licensing
Medical License Required
License number and issuing board or regulatory body. This is verified independently by BeautyJar. e.g. Georgia Composite Medical Board, License #MD-XXXXX
Board Certifications Required
All current board certifications relevant to the services offered. e.g. American Board of Dermatology; Korean Dermatological Association
Fellowships
Any relevant fellowships or professional society memberships. e.g. Fellow, American Academy of Dermatology (FAAD)
Education & Training Required
Medical school, residency, and any additional training programs. e.g. MD — Yonsei University College of Medicine; Residency — Samsung Medical Center Dermatology
Years in Practice
Approximate years of clinical practice. e.g. 15+ years
03
Services & Specialties
Primary Specialty Required
The main area of focus as it should appear on the profile. e.g. Medical Aesthetics & Skin Rejuvenation; Obesity Medicine & Metabolic Health
Services Offered Required
A list of specific treatments and services. Include as many as relevant — we'll organize them by category on the profile. e.g. Dermal Fillers, Neurotoxin Treatments, Laser Resurfacing, GLP-1 Weight Management, HRT, PRP & Microneedling
Telehealth Available?
Does the provider offer virtual consultations? If so, specify any geographic restrictions.
04
About & Bio
Existing Bio or Website Required
A link to the provider's current website bio, or an existing bio document. We will write the BeautyJar profile bio in our editorial voice based on this — the provider reviews and approves before publishing. e.g. https://yourpractice.com/about or attached document
Anything to Emphasize?
Is there anything specific the provider would like highlighted? A clinical philosophy, a unique approach, an area of particular expertise?
05
Practice Details
Accepting New Patients?
Is the practice currently accepting new patients? Any waitlist or scheduling notes?
Consultation Fee
How the initial consultation works — deposit required, complimentary, applied toward treatment, etc.
Payment Methods
Accepted payment types and any payment plan availability.
Cancellation Policy
Notice period and any fees for cancellations or no-shows.
06
Contact & Online Presence
Contact Email Required
The email address that should appear on the BeautyJar profile for patient inquiries. e.g. [email protected]
Website
Full URL of the practice website.
Instagram / Social
Primary social media handle(s) to display on the profile.
Office Contact Required
Name and email of the person at the practice who will be our point of contact for building the profile. This is not displayed publicly. e.g. Sarah, Office Manager — [email protected]
