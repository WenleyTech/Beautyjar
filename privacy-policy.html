Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 1, 2026

BeautyJar is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform — whether you are a consumer searching for care or a provider seeking to be listed.

1. Who We Are

BeautyJar™ is operated by Wenley Technologies and provides a verified medical wellness provider directory at beautyjar.com. We connect consumers with licensed, credentialed providers in GLP-1 weight management, hormone therapy, and non-invasive aesthetic care. References to "we," "us," or "BeautyJar" refer to BeautyJar™ / Wenley Technologies.

2. Information We Collect

From Consumers: When you browse the directory, we may collect basic usage data such as pages visited, search terms entered, and general location. We do not require account creation to browse.

From Providers: When you apply to be listed, we collect your name, practice name, professional license number, state of licensure, and contact information. Payment is processed securely through Stripe — we do not store your payment card details.

Automatically: We collect standard web analytics data including browser type, device type, referring URLs, and session duration. This data is aggregated and not linked to identifiable individuals.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify provider credentials and maintain listing accuracy
  • Operate and improve the directory and its search functionality
  • Communicate with providers about listings, subscription status, and platform updates
  • Send providers monthly marketing activity updates as described in our provider agreement
  • Comply with legal obligations and detect and prevent fraud or misuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Provider Profile Information

Provider profiles — including name, credentials, practice location, services, and contact information — are publicly visible on the BeautyJar directory by design. Providers consent to this visibility upon submitting their application and activating their listing.

5. Cookies and Tracking

BeautyJar uses cookies to support basic site functionality and analytics. These are not used to track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in the operation of BeautyJar:

  • Stripe — payment processing for provider subscriptions
  • Google Analytics — aggregated usage analytics
  • Google Forms / Google Workspace — provider application and communication

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain provider information for the duration of your active listing and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at providers@beautyjar.com. Providers wishing to remove their listing should contact us directly.

9. Children's Privacy

BeautyJar is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: providers@beautyjar.com
