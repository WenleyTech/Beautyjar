The Standard

What the Verified Badge Means

Every provider displaying the BeautyJar badge has passed an independent review of their license, identity, and practice. It is not self-reported. It is not automated. It is verified.

BeautyJar Verified

Independently reviewed · License confirmed · Identity verified · Practice validated

Why It Matters

A credential you can actually trust.

GLP-1 medical weight management, hormone therapy, and medical aesthetics are among the fastest-growing areas in women's health. They're also among the most inconsistently regulated. Providers range from highly credentialed specialists to practitioners with minimal training offering the same services.

The BeautyJar badge is a signal that a provider’s credentials have been reviewed and confirmed — not by an algorithm, not by reviews, but by a real verification process against official licensing records.

The Process

How we verify every provider.

01
License Confirmed

We verify active, valid licensure through official regulatory bodies in each provider’s jurisdiction. No expired licenses. No inactive registrations.

02
Identity Verified

The person behind the profile is confirmed to be the licensed practitioner they claim to be. Credentials are matched to the individual, not just the practice.

03
Practice Validated

The practice itself is reviewed — confirming it operates legitimately and that the services listed align with the provider’s verified scope of practice.
